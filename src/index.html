FAQ
What day is the best time
to move?

The best option is on Wednesday. Most often people try to move on Friday or at the weekend, so these days are very busy. Especially during the weekend there may be difficulties with access
to the elevator.

Can the weather prevent
the move?

Due to force majeure, serious traffic jams, there may be slight

What is the system
of payment for moving?

As a rule, we start from 2 options:
a fixed payment for moving or an hourly payment. Most often
we work in a fixed cost format. The same option is more
profitable for you.

How securely do you
pack all the things?

We select packaging according to the type of specific items: appliances, clothing, sculptures, fragile decor, mirrors, lamps.

pack a valuable work of art or your collection of heroes from
the Avengers.
